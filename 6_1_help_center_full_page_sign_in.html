How to create Shipping Storm account?

Follow Article

Borderlinx enables you to get around shipping restrictions once and for all. When an online retailer doesn’t ship to your country, just use your Borderlinx address.

In this article, you’ll learn how to register for a free account, and instantly get your own international forwarding addresses to use with online retailers.

Before you start

Here are some things to know before you begin this process.

You’ll need to provide an email address that will be associated with your account. We will use this email to communicate with you, so it should be an email address that you have access to.

All accounts are free, so you don’t need a credit card to sign up. When you choose to release your packages, we’ll need your payment method, just like any other ecommerce store. However, we can’t accept bank transfers, e-checks or checks as payment.

Sign up for an account

When you create a Borderlinx account, you agree to comply with our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.

1. Navigate to Shipping Storm Home page and click GET STARTED NOW or click REGISTER in the main menu.

2. On the Registration page, provide your Email, Password and Country, and click Register Now.

3. We'll display a confirmation message. Check your Inbox for the account activation email to complete your account setup.

The time it takes to receive the activation email can vary. If you haven't received the activation email, check your spam folder.
